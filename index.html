Our Purpose

Talent is Universal.
Opportunity is Not.

In every corner of our country, there are young minds with world-changing ideas—but without the zip code or network to bring them to life. NationsNext destroys these barriers.

We provide a professional, vetted platform where ambitious youth can showcase projects in STEM, Arts, Business, and Community Service directly to a network of verified donors and mentors.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is NationsNext a non-profit?

Yes, we are a registered 501(c)(3) organization. All donations made through the platform are tax-deductible.

How do you verify the projects?

We require school ID, parental consent (if under 18), and a teacher or community leader reference for every project submitted.

Does the platform take a fee?

NationsNext takes a 0% platform fee from student projects. We rely on separate "tipping" from donors to keep the lights on.
